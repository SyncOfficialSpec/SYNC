This batch introduces the Scripts app, a script browser that lives right on the SYNC dock.

New Elements:
Scripts App
A draggable macOS style window with a search field and a two column grid of script banners, powered by RScripts. Every card shows the script name, the game it is for and wallpaper art behind a soft dark fade, and clicking a card downloads the script and runs it instantly.

Search
Type in the search field and results appear on their own after a short pause, no button needed. Pressing enter searches instantly and the status line always tells you what you are looking at.

Infinite Scroll
Reaching the bottom of the grid quietly loads the next page of scripts, so you can keep scrolling through the whole catalog without ever pressing load more.

Card Badges
Verified creators get a blue VERIFIED pill on their banner and scripts locked behind a key system get an orange KEY pill, so you know what you are getting before you click.

Quick Copy
Right clicking any card copies a ready loadstring to your clipboard, handy for sharing a script or running it somewhere else.

Improvements:
Hovering a banner makes it grow smoothly with a soft shine sweeping across the art, and it never covers the cards next to it.
Cards fade and settle in one after another when the grid loads.
Executing a script pulses the card green on success and red on failure, with the result written in the status line.
The last loaded list is remembered, so reopening the app paints instantly instead of showing a loading screen.
The scrollbar is hidden for a cleaner look.
